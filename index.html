Creamy Garlic Pasta

A quick and easy pasta recipe loaded with flavor.

Ingredients: Pasta, garlic, cream, parmesan, olive oil.

Instructions: Boil pasta, sauté garlic in olive oil, add cream, mix with pasta, top with cheese.

Fresh Garden Salad

Crisp veggies tossed in a light vinaigrette.

Ingredients: Lettuce, tomato, cucumber, onion, olive oil, lemon juice.

Instructions: Chop veggies, mix in bowl, drizzle with dressing, toss and serve.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Rich, gooey chocolate dessert perfect for special nights.

Ingredients: Chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, flour.

Instructions: Melt chocolate and butter, mix with sugar and eggs, add flour, bake until gooey inside.
